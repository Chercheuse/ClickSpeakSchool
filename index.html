How is going the education?

1

The lessons are held on a convenient platform that allows for effective learning from any device and tracking of one's progress.

2

We build learning around your goals and interests. It is important to us that you enjoy learning English and study what is necessary and interesting for you..

3

We work with a communicative methodology that involves learning through communication (we introduce all new material into conversation).

4

Our goal is effective learning, so in our lessons, we work with all skills: speaking, grammar, vocabulary, reading, listening comprehension, pronunciation, and writing.

FAQ

In group lessons, there is even more conversation than in individual lessons. More people, more ideas, more communication. You discuss everything in pairs, with each other, rather than with the teacher one-on-one. Group and pair lessons are lively and usually quite fun. Experience shows that students learn more from each other than from the teacher. Moreover, group learning is more similar to real-life use of English.

As an English school, we understand the importance of maintaining progress in our students' learning journeys. While we do not encourage suspending learning altogether, we understand that there may be circumstances where it is necessary to do so.
However, in other cases, such as learning new information or skills that require ongoing practice and reinforcement, suspending learning may result in a loss of progress or even regression. It is important to assess the situation and the individual's learning needs before deciding whether to suspend or continue learning.

The duration of an English course can vary depending on the level of the course and the institution offering it. In general, English courses can range from a few weeks to several months or even a year, depending on the intensity and scope of the program.
